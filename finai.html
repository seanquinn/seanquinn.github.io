Finai

Predicting Stock Prices using Artificial Intelligence

Finai

Finance is a market ripe with information. Traders speculate on the movement of prices for individual symbols such as AAPL (Apple Inc.) with large volumes of captial. Artificialdev opened it's computer and thought 'Let's see what some Artificial Intelligence can do to predict stock prices'.

This project was an internal research effort, that investigated how historical market data could be used to determine future stock prices. Artificial Intelligence was employed through the use of Multi-layer Perceptron Neural Networks and Support Vector Machines. The project was deemed as a success by university professors, and could be used to inform investment decisions in equities. The project is currently in private beta due to the secretive nature of the algorithm.
