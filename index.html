About Us

A little history

Hey there! Welcome to Artificial Development's digital home. My name is Sean Quinn and I am the founder of the company. As Artificial Development is a startup, we do things a little differently around here. Our goal is to work closely with you to deliver you software that doesn't cost a fortune, while meeting all of your needs.

You won't see us rocking suits, but more than likely in a hoody with a cup of coffee working studiously on our laptops. Our expertise is in Software Development, Artificial Intelligence and Technical Consultation. We would love to work with your business and we're sure you won't regret it!

Blending Technology with Business

Here at Artificial Development, we believe in the power of software and how it can be used to maximise value for business stakeholders. That's why it is our mission to create powerful software that can improve your business potential.

Every project will involve direct contact with you, the customer, as well as providing regular demos to ensure that your project is going the way you want it to.
